Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really not use water for cleaning?

We don't use added water during the exterior-interior cleaning process! Our proprietary cleaning product biodegrades nearly 100%, doesn't burden the environment.

How long does the cleaning take?

An average size car's exterior and interior cleaning takes about 1 hour 20 minutes.

What areas in Abuja do you cover?

We cover all areas within Abuja including Maitama, Wuse, Garki, Asokoro, Gwarinpa, and surrounding areas.

What payment methods do you accept?

You can pay for the wash by bank card, bank transfer, or cash. You can select the payment method during booking in the app or on site.
