About WaxWorks

Get to know our eco-friendly car cleaning service

Our Story

WaxWorks is revolutionizing car cleaning in Abuja since 2023 with a clear focus on environmental friendliness and efficiency for individual car owners and corporate fleets. Our method, which saves over 99% of water compared to traditional vehicle cleaning, is revolutionary and helps dramatically reduce environmental footprint.

The WaxWorks mobile app makes vehicle management easier by allowing vehicle cleaning, care and preparation to be booked anywhere and at any time, without the vehicles having to be taken to an external service provider. This mobile service not only saves valuable time, but also reduces CO2 emissions as fewer trips are required for vehicle care and preparation.

Our Mission

To provide eco-friendly, convenient, and high-quality car cleaning services that save water, reduce carbon emissions, and make life easier for our customers in Abuja.

Our Vision

To become the leading eco-friendly mobile car wash service in Nigeria, revolutionizing how people think about car cleaning and environmental conservation.
